# Customization Plan

Goal: make opencore-tv feel purpose-built for the Hisense 100QD6QF.

## Keep

- Jellyfin
- YouTube
- HDMI/input shortcuts
- Settings access
- Clock/date

## Remove Or Hide

- App feeds
- Amazon clutter
- Store-like rows
- Unused categories
- Anything that makes the TV feel like a shopping surface

## First Design Direction

- Clean home with one hero/background area.
- Primary row: Jellyfin, YouTube, HDMI 1, HDMI 2, HDMI 3, HDMI 4, Inputs, Settings.
- Large focus states readable from couch distance.
- No paywalls, no telemetry, no online dependency for normal use.
