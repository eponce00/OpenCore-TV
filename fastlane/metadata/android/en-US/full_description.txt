OpenCore TV is a custom Android TV and Fire TV launcher focused on a clean home screen, high-resolution wallpapers, device-aware shortcuts, weather, and an integrated idle display.

Key features:

- Custom TV home screen with Favorites-first organization.
- HDMI/source tiles that can sit next to regular apps.
- Input label and icon personalization.
- Built-in wallpaper library and wallpaper rotation.
- Weather and clock widgets for both Home and Idle modes.
- Launcher-owned idle/screensaver experience.
- Launcher protection support for Fire TV home-button rescue behavior.

OpenCore TV is designed for couch-distance navigation with a remote: large focus targets, simple settings pages, and no ad/recommendation surface.
